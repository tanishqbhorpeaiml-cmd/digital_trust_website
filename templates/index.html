What is Digital Trust?

Digital Trust is the confidence users have in technology to perform securely, reliably, and ethically. It ensures that people and businesses can interact safely online, knowing their data and privacy are protected.

Why It Matters

  • Protects personal and financial data
  • Builds online confidence and transparency
  • Prevents digital fraud and misinformation

How to Build Digital Trust

  1. Use strong passwords and update them regularly
  2. Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)
  3. Check for HTTPS and verify domains
  4. Be alert to phishing emails and fake offers
  5. Keep software and antivirus updated
